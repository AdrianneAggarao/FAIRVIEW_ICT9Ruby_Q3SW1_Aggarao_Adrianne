Characters:

Grey's Anatomy

Summary



A medical based drama centered around Meredith Grey, an aspiring surgeon and daughter of one of the best surgeons, Dr. Ellis Grey. Throughout the series, Meredith goes through professional and personal challenges along with fellow surgeons at Seattle Grace Hospital.

CHARACTERS

Meredith Gray

Meredith is characterized as a "dark and twisty," broken woman who perceives the world in a variety of gray tones. She is an emotionally complicated person as a result of this. She is a careful observer of the people around her and is capable of understanding others when they are at their lowest points.


Derek Shepherd

Derek Shepherd, a well-known neurosurgeon and former head of neurosurgery at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital, served on the hospital's board of directors. He was married to Meredith Grey, with whom he has a son, Bailey, two daughters, Zola and Ellis. He was struck by a truck and later passed away from his wounds.

Miranda Bailey

General surgeon Miranda Bailey once served as the hospital's Chief of Surgery. She is married to Ben Warren and has a kid named Tuck from a previous relationship with Tucker Jones. She was shy and lacked confidence throughout her internship at Seattle Grace Hospital until Richard Webber became her mentor.

Christina Yang

Strong, self-assured, and never afraid to speak her opinion. Cristina Yang is determined, extremely smart, and ambitious. She isn't hesitant to display her skills because she is aware of her potential to excel as a physician at Seattle Grace.
